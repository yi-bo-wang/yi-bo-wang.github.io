About

I am a research intern in the Chiral Representation Theory Unit at the Okinawa Institute of Science and Technology, working with Prof. Tomoyuki Arakawa.

My current interests include vertex algebras, affine W-algebras, BRST cohomology, Slodowy slices, arc spaces, representation theory, and mathematical aspects of conformal field theory.

I completed my BSc in Mathematics at King’s College London. I will begin Part III in Theoretical Physics at the University of Cambridge in October 2026.
