About

I am a research intern in the Chiral Representation Theory Unit at the Okinawa Institute of Science and Technology, supervised by Prof. Tomoyuki Arakawa.

My current interests are in mathematical physics and theoretical physics, especially vertex operator algebras, representation theory, and algebraic, geometric, and topological structures in quantum field theory.

I completed my BSc in Mathematics at King’s College London. I will begin Part III in Theoretical Physics at the University of Cambridge in October 2026.
